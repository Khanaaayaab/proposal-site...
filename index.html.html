Sometimes in life…
Allah sends someone special…
Someone who changes everything.

The day you came into my life,
happiness found its way to my heart.

Every moment with you feels like a blessing.
And I realized…
I don’t want just moments with you…
I want a lifetime.

With respect and sincerity…
I want to ask you something very important.

Will You Accept Me For Nikkah?

Will you walk with me in this journey of life
as my partner, my peace, and my forever?

Alhamdulillah ❤️
May Allah bless our Nikkah and our future together.
